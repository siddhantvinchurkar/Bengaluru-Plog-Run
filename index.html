What Is It About?

Bengaluru Plog Run is an initiative by Go Native that calls out to all citizens of Bengaluru.

The increasing amount of plastic use and improper plastic waste disposal has steadily been choking the environment and Bengaluru, as a city, has been making constant efforts to actively bring about a change.

But, the STIGMA attached to the act of picking up garbage has been enforced for centuries and is now fixated heavily within us - making us think twice before we pick up a piece of plastic that has been littered all over, sometimes even by us.

Next

What are we going to do about it?

Encourage local communities to walk together for change.

Bengaluru Plog Run will mark localities in the city of Bengaluru and work towards bringing a change from within the local communities. Within a marked out radius of 3-5 km of your neighbourhood, we encourage you to sign up, walk with us on the 2nd of October and

BREAK THE STIGMA by picking up dry plastic waste that has been littered. All you need to do is - click a button and fill a form and we will provide you with the rest, from a plog run route in your area all the way to kits that will help you pick the plastic.
