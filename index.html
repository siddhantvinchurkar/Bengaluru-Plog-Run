What are we going to do about it?

Encourage local communities to walk together for change.

Bengaluru Plog Run will mark localities in the city of Bengaluru and work towards bringing a change from within the local communities. Within a marked out radius of 3-5 km of your neighbourhood, we encourage you to sign up, walk with us on the 2nd of October and

BREAK THE STIGMA by picking up dry plastic waste that has been littered. All you need to do is - click a button and fill a form and we will provide you with the rest, from a plog run route in your area all the way to kits that will help you pick the plastic.

Meet the creators.

Bengaluru plog Run is an initiative brought to you by the following organizations.

Go Native

Go Native is a quiet little retreat with a Farm to Table restaurant and lifestyle store in Bangalore. Go Native aims to bring the Sustainable products into our daily living

Namma Nimma Cycle Foundation

A not for profit company focussed on sustainable mobility and bringing the culture of cycling into urban areas.

United Way Bengaluru

UWBe focuses its efforts on education, livelihood, health and environment. UWBe seeks to engage with and empower local communities.

Let's Be The Change

Let's Be The Change, a youth driven NGO run with a vision for a cleaner country. Over 5 years LBTC has fixed over 300 garbage dumps with over 1000 volunteers. LBTC is a bridge connection between the Municipal Corporation (BBMP) and the Citizens.
