How do you Plog?

Equip yourself with the right running gear and a bag to drop waste into.

Run along a designate route with peers carrying the bag along.

Pick plastic waste along the route and drop into the bag; continue running.

At the end of the route, accumulate all collected waste for segregation.

Meet the creators.

Bengaluru plog Run is an initiative brought to you by the following organizations.

Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike

The Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike, is the administrative body responsible for the civic and infrastructural assets of the Greater Bangalore metropolitan area.

Go Native

Go Native is a concept store and restaurant rooted in sustainable living. It is an initiative seeks to make eating, living, and playing sustainably a practical, hands-on experience that is beautiful, fun and utterly delicious. Go Native sources directly from the grassroots, ensuring that every product, every ingredient, benefits the livelihoods of the farmers and artisans who make them.

Namma Nimma Cycle Foundation

A not for profit company focussed on sustainable mobility and bringing the culture of cycling into urban areas.

United Way Bengaluru

UWBe focuses its efforts on education, livelihood, health and environment. UWBe seeks to engage with and empower local communities.

Let's Be The Change

Let's Be The Change, a youth driven NGO run with a vision for a cleaner country. Over 5 years LBTC has fixed over 300 garbage dumps with over 1000 volunteers. LBTC is a bridge connection between the Municipal Corporation (BBMP) and the Citizens.
