Who are we?

Average Bangaloreans frustrated because of the plastic menace.

Bengaluru as a city alone contributes 313.87 tonnes of plastic to the 25,000 tonnes of plastic that is produced by India as a country. Keeping in mind the fact that a lot of this waste is not disposed properly, the plastic ends up choking water and soil in its vicinity, ensuring a harmful degradation of the environment.

Bengaluru Plog Run is an initiative that encourages and helps spread awareness through our local communities. Together, we can learn how plastic can be disposed the right way and also, take a small steps to make sure we reduce our plastic usage.
